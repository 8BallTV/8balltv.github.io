8 Ball TV will attempt to air all submissions, however, we reserve the right to refuse or remove videos that are deemed to be in violation of the 8 Ball Community mission. The promotion of racism, sexism, homophobia, intolerance, xenophobia, or nonconsensual behavior are in direct violation of the 8 Ball Community mission. In these cases, we will attempt to contact the video's creator for an explanation, but due to the volume of our library, this cannot be guaranteed.
Any questions: 8balltelevision@gmail.com

....8 Ball TV is an online public access station.

....Everyone is invited to participate, either by submitting original content or filming in our NY studio.

....We will provide equipment, support, and studio time as needed to the best of our ability.

....You can send a one-off video or host a regular show.

....We will attempt to air everything that is submitted.

....No commercial or branded videos allowed.

....You will retain and own the rights to your content.

....8 Ball TV is a platform for and exists to support community broadcasting.

....We do not intend to censor anyone and content may be submitted anonymously.
