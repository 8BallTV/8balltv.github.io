Submission Standards

    8 Ball TV will attempt to air all submissions, however, we reserve the right to refuse or remove videos that are deemed to be in violation of the 8 Ball Community mission. The promotion of racism, sexism, homophobia, intolerance, xenophobia, or nonconsensual behavior are in direct violation of the 8 Ball Community mission.
